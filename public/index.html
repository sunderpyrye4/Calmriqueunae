Company Calmriqueunae creates this harmony through professional selection of textiles and lighting in style home decor , transforming ordinary interiors into atmospheric and inspiring spaces.

We believe that every home is unique, as are those who live in it. Therefore, our team approaches the work as subtly and individually as possible, exploring not only the style of the room, but also the lifestyle of the owners. Thanks to this, the interior receives a soul, and every detail works to create feelings of coziness, comfort and aesthetic pleasure.

Textiles are one of those details that can completely change the perception of space. It adds volume, softness, depth and even character. As part of our services, we carefully consider every element:

  • Curtains and tulle
    Soft and airy fabrics create lightness, muted shades add calm, and dense textures protect space and create an atmosphere of privacy.
  • Bedspreads and blankets
    They create comfort and invite relaxation. We select them so that they harmoniously combine with the furniture, wall shades and the general mood of the entire interior.
  • Decorative pillows
    Small accents - big emotions. Pillows allow you to change the mood of a room without making major changes and add layers to the interior.
  • Upholstery fabrics
    We select materials for furniture, combining practicality, tactility and aesthetics. Each fabric creates its own story: from elegant velor to structured linen.

Working with textiles, we create the effect of completeness and integrity of the space. We can say that textiles are the final touch that makes the interior lively, warm and full of character.

ðŸ’¡ Light as the basis of mood

Lighting is more than just functionality. This is the language with which the interior speaks to us. Our mission is to select lighting solutions so that they highlight the architecture, enhance emotions and create unique lighting scenarios.

We work with several levels of light:

1. General light

It forms basic lighting, making the space comfortable for everyday tasks. We select chandeliers and ceiling lamps that are in harmony with the interior style and do not overload the space.

2. Accent light

Sconces, pendants, floor lamps are elements that create the mood. They not only illuminate, but also highlight areas, add depth and form the composition of the interior.

3. Decorative light

This is the light that creates an atmosphere of magic. Soft lighting, designer lighting elements - all this turns an ordinary evening into a cozy ritual.

Properly planned lighting allows you to change the mood of a space with one movement of the hand. Thanks to this, the interior becomes alive, flexible and adapts to the rhythm of life.
