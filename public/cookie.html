1. Understanding Cookies

Cookies constitute minor data files deposited onto your computing device by web domains you access. Their widespread application facilitates streamlined website operation, enriches user interactions, and enables the provision of tailored digital content. These digital markers are categorized as follows:

  • Temporary (Session) Cookies: These ephemeral files are active solely for the duration of your browsing session, ceasing to exist upon browser closure or logout.
  • Permanent (Persistent) Cookies: These cookies persist on your device for a predetermined timeframe or until manually expunged by the user.
  • Proprietary (First-party) Cookies: Originating directly from the specific website you are currently navigating.
  • External (Third-party) Cookies: Issued by entities distinct from the website operator, often employed by advertisers or analytics providers to gauge user activities and inclinations.

3. Administering Your Cookie Settings

The prerogative to adjust your cookie preferences rests with you at any juncture. The majority of web browsers furnish tools within their configuration menus to regulate, inhibit, or eradicate cookies. It is imperative to acknowledge that the deactivation of cookies may impinge upon your capacity to utilize specific functionalities offered by our website. Below are illustrative instructions for managing cookies across prevalent web browsers:

  1. Google Chrome: Navigate to Settings > Privacy and security > Site Settings > Cookies and site data.
  2. Mozilla Firefox: Access Menu > Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data.
  3. Apple Safari: Proceed to Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data.
  4. Microsoft Edge: Go to Settings > Cookies and site permissions.
